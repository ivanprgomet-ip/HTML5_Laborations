Dusit Thani Maldives

Dusit Thani Maldives blends graceful Thai hospitality with the unparalleled luxury setting of the Maldives. Encircled by white sandy beaches, a stunning 360 degree living house reef and a turquoise lagoon, this luxury resort on Mudhdhoo Island in Baa Atoll – the Maldives’ first ever UNESCO World Biosphere Reserve – is just 35 minutes by seaplane from the capital city Malé and 10 minutes by speedboat from the Dharavandhoo Domestic Airport in Baa Atoll.

The natural beauty of the Maldives forms the perfect backdrop for the facilities and activities on offer at Dusit Thani Maldives. Guests can dive and snorkel, swim in one of the Maldives’ largest infinity pools, retreat to one of Devarana Spa’s treetop treatment pods for a pampering experience or dine at one of the exquisite restaurants before heading back to the comfort of one of the Thai-inspired villas or residences.

Welcome to Mudhdhoo Island

Activities

Encircled by a rich house reef and located close to Hanifaru Bay, Dusit Thani Maldives is the perfect jump off point to discover the majestic underwater world. On the surface, experience adrenaline-fuelled water sports, or go on a sunset cruise in search of spinner dolphins. Play tennis or get fit in the well-equipped fitness centre. Meditate with our resident yoga instructor in our yoga pavilion. Explore how the resort operates in a sustainable manner by going on a behind-the-scenes tour, and meet our marine biologist who leads the resort’s conservation programmes. For a taste of traditional island life, visit a nearby inhabited island for afternoon tea with the locals.

Devarana Spa

Devoted to premium treatments focused on pampering and wellness, Devarana Spa is a place where age-old Thai therapies meet modern knowledge. Elegant décor and the soothing tones of Thai classical music signal guests’ entry into an environment where luxury and elegance blend with the Thai heritage of natural healing.
