About the hobby

What?

My favourite hobby (atm) is to code and spend time with my computer. When coding, me and my computer don't always get along but thats part of the code-game. The coding includes C#, Html, SQL etc.

Why?

Coding is my favourite hobby because I love solving problems through trial and error. The feeling of a solved programatic problem is huge when the process of solving it has involved deep focus of the problem together with trial and error.

Other hobbies?

I like to work out at the gym, but i don't consider it a direct hobby. It's more of a duty to stay healthy (because coding, generally, involves alot of sitting).
